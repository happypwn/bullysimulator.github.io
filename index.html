THE GAME

Take the role of Charlie and become a master of physics and the gravity tilt to beat the challenging puzzles!

The challenge

Tilt the world on its shoulder and run up the side of a building while wrapping your head around how to get to the next area. Flint and Charlie is going to challenge your perception of room and space by turning it all upside down!

The fun

In Flint and Charlie you will use physics, fine control and timing to reach battle the platforming challenges laid out infront of you.
