THE STORY

Take the role of Charlie, a troubled teenager who's getting some grown up life experience by working with his eccentric scientist uncle, Professor Flint. The two of you go away on a dubious scavengehunt to raid an abandoned deep space research station. Things do not go as planned and soon you find yourself in quite the pickle, of galactic proportions!

Conquer gravity

Use an arsenal of "legitimately scientific" gadgets to shoot blackholes, create rifts in gravity, and fun stuff that would make Newton roll over in his grave.

Puzzles and platforming

Flint and Charlie: A Galactic Pickle is a Puzzle-platformer where you use physics, gravity, fine control and timing to reach otherwise impossible goals.
